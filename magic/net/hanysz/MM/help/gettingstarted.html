Getting started

Magic Metronome will play a sequence of sounds according to a script. Try loading a script from the "Examples" menu, then press the "Play" button to see what it sounds like.

As well as doing the things you'd expect from a normal metronome, Magic Metronome can also play scripts with changing time signatures, rests and pauses, sudden or gradual tempo changes, and even polyphonic scripts with two or more tracks simultaneously.

You can edit the example scripts and customise them for your own uses, or you can learn to write your own scripts from scratch.

You can learn about all the features of Magic Metronome simply by looking at the example scripts, or you can read the rest of the help pages for a more systematic explanation.

As you read through the help pages, you might like to copy and paste the examples into the script window and play them.
